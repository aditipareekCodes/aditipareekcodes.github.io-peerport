Our Mission

Developed exclusively for the A. P. Shah Institute of Technology, PeerPort was created to solve a common engineering problem: the financial burden of academic resources. Our platform eliminates "dead stock" by allowing seniors to seamlessly pass down textbooks, drafters, and tools to juniors in a secure, hyper-local environment.
